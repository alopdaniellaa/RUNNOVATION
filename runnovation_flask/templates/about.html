About Runnovation

Runnovation is a desktop-focused web application that combines Strava route data with community feedback to support safer and smarter running decisions in Urdaneta City.

How Strava Data is Used

  • Pace, distance, elevation, and frequency are imported from Strava activities.
  • Route-based analytics (difficulty, elevation profiles, trends) are derived from Strava data only.
  • All screens that use Strava data show labels like “Data Source: Strava API”.

Which Data Belongs to Runnovation?

  • Route feedback (difficulty and safety ratings).
  • User comments and community insights.
  • Usability feedback (SUS / SUMI) and system usage metrics.
  • These are always labeled as “Runnovation App Data” or “User-Submitted Data”.

SDG Alignment

  • SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-Being (promoting active lifestyles).
  • SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure (data-driven route planning).
  • SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities (safer, more informed mobility).
